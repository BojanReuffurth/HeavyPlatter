VinCo

VINYL COLLECTION TRACKER FOR IOS


About VinCo

VinCo is a vinyl record collection tracker for iPhone. Catalogue your records, track condition and value, browse tracklists, and manage a wishlist — everything stored privately on your device.

VinCo is free with no paywalled features. If you'd like to support development, a voluntary tip jar is available inside the app under Settings → Support.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a record to my collection?

+

Tap the + button on the Collection tab. You can enter details manually, or use the search tool to automatically fetch album metadata, cover art, and tracklists from MusicBrainz and iTunes.

Is my collection data backed up?

+

Your collection is stored locally on your iPhone. It will be included in your iCloud device backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled in iPhone Settings → [your name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup. VinCo does not run its own backup service. You can also export to CSV from the app as a manual backup.

What happens if I delete the app?

+

Deleting VinCo removes all locally stored data. If you have an iCloud device backup or a CSV export saved, you can restore your collection by reinstalling the app and re-importing the CSV file.

How does CSV export work?

+

Go to Settings → Export. VinCo generates a CSV file containing all your records and wishlist items. You can save it to Files, share via AirDrop, or email it to yourself. The same file can be re-imported into VinCo later.

Does VinCo need an internet connection?

+

No. Your existing collection is always accessible offline. An internet connection is only needed when fetching album metadata, cover art, tracklists, or pricing from external services.

Is there a subscription? What is the tip jar?

+

There is no subscription and no locked features. The tip jar (Settings → Support) is a fully voluntary one-time in-app purchase to support ongoing development. You are never required to tip to use any part of the app.

How do I connect Discogs?

+

Go to Settings → Connect. From there you can optionally link your Discogs account for pricing and marketplace data. This is entirely optional — VinCo works fully without it.
